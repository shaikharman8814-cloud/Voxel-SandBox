HOW TO PLAY

  • Survival: Keep an eye on your hunger! Moving depletes it. If it hits 0, you lose health. Fall damage is also active!
  • Mining: Hold Left Click to mine. Harder blocks like Stone take longer to break than Dirt.
  • Crafting: Press 'E' to open inventory. Drag 4 Wood Planks (ID: 6) into the 2x2 grid to make a Crafting Table.
  • Zombies: Every 60 seconds, a hostile Zombie will spawn nearby. It will hunt you down!
  • TNT: Select TNT, place it, and Right-Click it to ignite. Stand back!
